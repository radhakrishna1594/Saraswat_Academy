About Saraswat Academy

Saraswat Academy is a free educational platform dedicated to helping CBSE students from Class 6 to 12 through high-quality NCERT Solutions, Notes, MCQs, Worksheets, Practice Papers, Previous Year Questions and easy-to-understand study material. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to every student regardless of financial background.

🎯 Our Mission


Our mission is to simplify education and help students build strong conceptual understanding instead of rote learning. Every article published on Saraswat Academy is designed to be simple, reliable and useful for school examinations as well as competitive exams.
